App Name: QR Pro - Professional QR Code Studio

Description
Unleash the Power of QR Codes with QR Pro.

QR Pro is the ultimate all-in-one suite for creating, tracking, and managing high-performance QR codes. Designed for both business and personal use, it combines advanced customization with deep analytics to help you connect with your audience like never before.

Key Features:
* Generate with Precision: Create beautiful QR codes for URLs and professional Digital Contact Cards (vCards) in seconds.
* Branding & Style: Stand out from the crowd! Customize foreground and background colors, and embed your own high-resolution PNG logo directly into the code.
* Dynamic Animation: Go beyond static images. Generate eye-catching Animated GIF QR codes that demand attention on digital screens.
* Deep Analytics Dashboard: Know your audience. Track total scans, identify user devices (Mobile vs. Desktop), and monitor scan activity over time with real-time charts.
* Advanced QR Viewer: Decipher any QR code instantly. Our smart scanner handles low-light and sideways photos, giving you immediate access to hidden data or clickable links.
* Digital Business Cards: Simplify networking. Generate vCards that users can scan to save your contact info directly to their phones.
* Secure & Private: Our "Secure Link" technology ensures your tracking URLs stay professional and protected.
* Privacy First: Built-in expiration dates allow you to create time-sensitive codes that automatically deactivate when you choose.

Whether you're a marketing professional looking for scan statistics or a networking pro sharing contact details, QR Pro is the only QR tool you'll ever need.
